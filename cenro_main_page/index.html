Welcome to the City Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO) official website. CENRO is dedicated to promoting sustainable urban development and preserving natural resources through comprehensive planning, conservation initiatives, and community engagement.

MISSION

The City Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO) plays a crucial role in promoting sustainable development and the preservation of natural resources within urban areas. Through its strategic initiatives and policies, CENRO strives to create a harmonious balance between urbanization and environmental conservation. By addressing issues such as waste management, air quality, and green spaces, CENRO contributes to the overall well-being and resilience of cities, ensuring a healthier and greener future for its residents Through collaborative efforts, CENRO ensures a greener and healthier future for cities and their residents.
